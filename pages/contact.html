FAQ

Common Questions

Is the consultation really free?

Yes — your first 30-minute consultation is completely free and carries no obligation. It's a chance for you to understand your legal options and for me to assess your case.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. Everything discussed during a consultation is protected by attorney-client privilege. Nothing you share will ever be disclosed without your consent.

How quickly can you take on my case?

For urgent matters (bail, injunctions, protection orders), I can act within 24 hours. For non-urgent cases, we'll schedule at a mutually convenient time.

What are your fees?

Fees vary by case type and complexity. I offer transparent, upfront pricing discussed during the consultation — no hidden charges. Flexible payment plans are available.

Do you handle cases outside your city?

Yes — I appear at the High Court and can handle cases across multiple districts. For outstation cases, consultations can be conducted via phone or video call.

Can I speak to you directly or will I be passed to a junior?

As a solo practitioner, you work directly with me at every stage of your case. You will always have my direct contact and personal attention.
